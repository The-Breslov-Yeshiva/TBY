Under the Guidance of Rabbi Nissan Dovid Kivak, Shlita

Rabbi Nissan Dovid Kivak, shlita, stands as one of the most influential leaders in the global Breslov community. With over five decades of dedicated service, he has transformed countless lives through his profound Torah teachings and unwavering commitment to spreading Rebbe Nachman's path.

His visionary leadership has established numerous educational institutions worldwide, authored essential sefarim on Breslov teachings, and created thriving communities that continue to inspire spiritual seekers globally. The Breslov Yeshiva is privileged to operate under his direct guidance and blessing.
